# Thanks for the Buff

Automatically express appreciation with emotes and messages whenever you receive a buff — whether it's a stranger buffing you in the open world or a teammate popping a cooldown for you in combat.

## What It Does

In the heat of battle, it's easy to miss that clutch Power Infusion or a life-saving Lay on Hands. Thanks for the Buff keeps you informed when teammates use their cooldowns for you, and automatically reacts with a random emote when a stranger buffs you out in the world — all without you having to lift a finger.

## Features

**Buffs from Strangers** — When a friendly player outside your group buffs you, TFTB can automatically perform a random emote (Cheer, Thank, Salute, etc.) to show your appreciation. Includes a configurable cooldown to prevent spam and a minimum buff duration filter to ignore short heals over time like Renew or Rejuvenation.

**Combat Buff Tracking** — Tracks major cooldowns from your party and raid members across all classes: Innervate, Power Infusion, Misdirection, Hand of Protection, Tricks of the Trade, and many more. Prints a notification in your chat so you always know who's looking out for you. Optionally whisper your benefactor instead.

**The Thank You Button** — Automatically creates a macro named `- Thank` on login. Target a player and press it to instantly send a personalized whisper and a random emote — perfect for thanking healers, mages for water, or warlocks for portals.

**Item Tracking** — Recognizes party items like Drums of Battle, Drums of Speed, and their Greater variants, notifying you when a group member uses one for the party.

**Localized** — Works with all Classic WoW Locales (enUS, deDE, esES, esMX, frFR, itIT, koKR, ptBR, ruRU, zhCN, zhTW).

## Settings

Type `/tftb` in-game to open the options panel. From here you can:

<img width="800" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0df2e8a3-b05d-4e8d-8846-f4de6c454eef" />

**Buffs from Strangers** — Toggle the feature on or off, set the cooldown between thank-yous to the same player, set a minimum buff duration to filter out short HoTs, choose your messaging preference (silent, self-only chat message, or whisper), and pick which emotes to use.

**Combat Buffs** — Choose your messaging preference and toggle tracking for individual abilities on a per-spell basis.

**Thank You Button** — Toggle automatic macro creation, customize your whisper message, and select which emotes the button uses.

### Slash Commands

| Command | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| `/tftb` | Open the options panel |
| `/thankyou` | Emote and whisper your targeted player |
